The story of a mid-summer-night dream into reality!

10th April, 2016! The day when D’pM ORIGINALS was born. A dream of a civil engineer, turned into reality. Initially, D’pM ORIGINALS was started by Mr. Divyanshu Mishra For 6 months, he worked alone on his photography start-up, which he wanted to take at a professional level. After 6 months of sleepless nights and sweat breaks, finally, his work caught the lime light, and slowly the baby D’pM started to grow, crawl and walk. Eventually, he got 3 more people to work with him and today, this initiative is indeed a famous ‘Brand-Name’. Ranging from Professional photo shoots to wedding photography, from getting picture perfect frames to making your photo speak a thousand words for yourself, D’pM ORIGINALS is indeed a perfect choice for all the events you’re looking for. Every sector has different demand for their ‘picture perfect’ moments. Youth want candid to speak for them, kids want something which will just make them smile and of course, the elder and wise sector of the society looks for a nostalgic classic portrait! Whatever your desire and imagination is, D’pM ORIGINALS knows the subtle art of printing your thoughts down onto the screen!
