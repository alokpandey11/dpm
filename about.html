ABOUT DIVYANSHU

Hi There...

Hey everybody! I’m Divyanshu Mishra, Founder, and CEO of D’pM ORIGINALS and D’pM ASSOCIATES. I’m an entrepreneur by profession, photographer by passion, and an engineer by choice. I have worked with several projects, which are extremely diverse and beautiful, for the past few years. From capturing the priceless moments of the people to re-defining memories, I have been to various places, experienced different emotions, and redefined the idea of ‘being on the other side of the lens’ in a personalized way! Hence all of my projects are like my own personal tasks, which I happily do. Honestly, I have a clear aim to strive for perfection, to turn ideas on paper into reality, to bring a smile on the face and glitter in the eyes of the people through my work. Since a picture speaks a thousand words, I hope my passion is visible, through the photographs, projects, and other contents on this website.
